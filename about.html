About Me

Bio

my name is Karimah Ahmad a builder and an educator who transitioned into tech. I started up with product management, but curiosity led me to study something outside product. Here i am a Frontend Developer. I'm Kaduna born and bred, currently living in Bauchi State. I'm married and blessed with three amazing kids. My professional journey has been a mix of learning, building, and evolving. From the classroom to tech spaces, I've continued to push myself to grow, explore new ideas, and create solutions that make a difference. I'm passionate about innovation, collaboration, and helping others find their path, just like I found mine.

Goals in This Program

My goal in the HNG program is to stretch beyond my comfort zone and gain hands-on experience that sharpens both my technical and soft skills. I want to deepen my understanding of real-world problem-solving, learn to collaborate effectively in diverse teams, and deliver results that speak to innovation and quality. I also aim to build a strong network of like-minded innovators while improving my confidence in executing tasks independently and creatively.

Areas of Low Confidence

I also tend to overthink my outputs, worrying if they meet expectations. However, i am learning to trust the process to ask questions, seek feedback early, and focus on progress rather than perfection.
